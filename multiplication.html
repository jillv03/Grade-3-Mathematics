What is multiplication?

Multiplication is the process of calculating the product of two or more numbers. In Maths, the basic explanation of multiplication is reapeted addition. For Example, 3 x 5 is equals to 15. Multiplying 3 to 5 can be shown as 3 + 3 + 3 + 3 + 3 and you have to add the reapeted numbers that is equals to 15 or it can be shown as 5 + 5 + 5 that is equal to 15.





Multiplication consist four parts. The operation symbol, multiplier, multiplicand, and the product. In multiplication, the alignment of numbers has not any rules.

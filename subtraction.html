What is Subtraction?

Subtraction is a process of getting the difference of two numbers or integer. Just like adding numbers but the opposite and instead of adding, taking away number is the purposed of subtraction. Subtraction symbol is denoted as "-"





Subtraction consist four different parts. The operation symbol, minuend, subtrahend, and the difference. In subtracting numbers, you have to align numbers to get the proper equation.

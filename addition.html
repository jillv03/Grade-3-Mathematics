What is addtion?

Addition is the term used to describe adding two or more numbers together. The sign for Addition is called plus which denoted as "+" and result of adding two or more numbers is called the sum.





In addition, it has three parts. The addens, opreation symbol, and the total or sum of the equation. In adding numbers, you have to align numbers in right form to make it easier to solve the equation.
